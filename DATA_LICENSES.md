## Amazon Reviews 2023

The review and item metadata are third-party research data published by
McAuley Lab. They are **not** relicensed by this repository. Users must follow
the source publisher's terms and cite the dataset paper/project page. The raw
files, review text, images and user identifiers are not committed here.

Derived aggregate tables and charts are included for portfolio review. They do
not contain full review text or raw user identifiers.
